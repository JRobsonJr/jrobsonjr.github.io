A little about me

I'm a 22-year-old Brazilian with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science by UFCG. During my undergraduate years, I had experiences in a handful of projects, which had me working with web development, researching, testing, and even a little data science and natural language processing.

Currently, I'm a Software Engineer at VTEX, working in the Analytics team, where I'm focused on frontend development. There, I help develop data and metric-related platforms, such as the Sales Performance Dashboard.

I'd like to think that I come off as a well-tempered, easy going person. Of course, you have no option but to believe me, since I could write anything here. But really, feel free to contact me. I don't bite!

I also love writing and I'm always doing it, consciously or not. To check out my writing, you could either read some of my poetry attempts or check out some technical articles I've written in the past.
