About me

I was born 21 years ago in Patos, a small town in the state of Paraíba, Brazil. At 17, I moved to Campina Grande, where I'm pursuing a degree in Computer Science at the Federal University of Campina Grande (UFCG). In the meantime, I've had experience in a few projects which gave me experience with web development, researching, testing, and even a little data science.

Currently, I'm a software engineer intern at the partnership between VTEX and UFCG, developing modern web applications with technologies such as React and Node.js.

I also dedicate part of my free time to my work as a member of the core team of OpenDevUFCG, an organization created to help spread the culture of open source in the university environment. There I've helped maintain projects such as a blog with CS-related topics, manage social media accounts and organize events, including a local Hacktoberfest event. That's where this photo of me was taken!

I'm also one of the creators of an open-source music-loving organization called Call Us What You Want, but more on that below.
